Research

My empirical work on public finance and data.

Heterodata

The research-data platform: a hub of interactive data sites including Gerhard (US public finance, gerhard.heterodata.org), Shaikh Data (shaikh.heterodata.org — replications of Anwar Shaikh’s Capitalism and Measuring the Wealth of Nations), Lewis (international economics, lewis.heterodata.org), Leonid (Soviet statistics, leonid.heterodata.org), Leontief (US input–output, leontief.heterodata.org), and StarCruiser (US employment and inflation, starcruiser.heterodata.org), plus affiliated standalone sites: freenic.org (banking data), foodberg.org (food prices), hmdaexplore.com (mortgage lending), methodex.fyi (statistical methodology), and jjmuni.com (municipal pedestrian infrastructure). Data downloads across the ecosystem are gathered at files.heterodata.org and indexed on the hub’s data page.

Economics at the New School

HMDA Explore

An interactive explorer over Home Mortgage Disclosure Act data, in two versions side by side:

  • andenick.shinyapps.io/HMDAExplore — the earlier interactive draft (R-Shiny; slower to start).
  • hmdaexplore.com — the actively developed web version.
